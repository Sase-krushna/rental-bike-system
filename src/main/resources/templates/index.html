About RideShare

RideShare started with a simple idea: getting around the city shouldn't require owning a vehicle. We built a rental platform that makes it easy to grab a bike, ride where you need to go, and return it without any hassle.

Our fleet is carefully maintained and inspected regularly, so every ride is safe and smooth. Whether you're commuting to work, exploring the city, or just need a bike for an hour, we've got you covered — with transparent, hourly pricing and no hidden fees.
