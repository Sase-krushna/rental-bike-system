About RideShare

RideShare started with a simple idea: getting around the city shouldn't require owning a vehicle. We built a rental platform that makes it easy to grab a bike, ride where you need to go, and return it without any hassle.

Our fleet is carefully maintained and inspected regularly, so every ride is safe and smooth. Whether you're commuting to work, exploring the city, or just need a bike for an hour, we've got you covered — with transparent, hourly pricing and no hidden fees.

Rental Terms

Security Deposit

We ask a security deposit on all bikes or cycling gear you rent. You can make a payment via a debit or credit card. We cannot return your money until the gear is returned.

Age Restrictions

If you want to rent a bike, you have to be at least 18 years old. Don't forget to bring your driver's license. Younger participants need to be accompanied by a parent or guardian.

Helmets

Additionally, we provide all our clients with mandatory helmets fitted right to your size. Be careful on the road!

Damage

If you want to rent a bike or any cycling gear, you will be responsible for it until the time of return. Theft and any possible damage are included.

Riding Rules

All renters have to stick to traffic rules, signs, laws, and regulations during the riding trip. It is advisable not to use smartphones while riding.

Returning Equipment

Return your bike or gear at least 15 minutes prior to closing time to check it out. We usually work till 8pm on weekdays and 6pm on Sundays.

If you have any questions or want to rent a bike, feel free to get in touch with us.
